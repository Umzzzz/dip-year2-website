A student guide to sports in Manchester

Sports in Manchester is an important part of the city's culture. From Old Trafford Stadium to the Manchester Velodrome, the city is full of exciting sporting opportunities that everybody is able to get involved in. With there being over 85,000 students in Manchester alone, what sports are available for students on a budget?

We have researched what is available in the city, and narrowed them down to a few top sports where you will find the best venues with the best prices. Here is a guide to some of the top sports that you can get yourself involved in while living in Manchester, including Football, Basketball, Cricket, Tennis and Judo.
